Time and habits blog.

I looked into the "Pomodoro Technique" and also read about meditation and growth vs. fixed mindsets.

I found the pomodoro technique interesting, this could be a good way for me to schedule breaks rather than just taking random ones. I often find myself getting frustrated with something if I dont take more regular breaks so this could be a good strategy to impliment. Also the fixed vs growth mindset blog was facinating, I think I was able to develop into a growth mindset during my unversity studies through developing good work habits.

I think the meditation technique would suit me best, I need to unwind completely and clear my mind otherwise during breaks I would keep thinking about the work I just completed or have yet to attempt.

I think I will try to implement the Pomodoro technique and see how I go with more regular breaks but I feel like meditation will be most beneficial to my learning style.
